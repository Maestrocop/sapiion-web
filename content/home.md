## PROBLEM SECTION

**Section header:**
Education runs on disconnected tools. That has a cost.

**Body:**
Most institutions use five or six separate systems — one for curriculum, one for teaching, one for grades, one for attendance, one for student progress. None of them talk to each other.

The result:
- Coordinators discover students are at risk only after grades have already failed
- Teachers grade assignments that are disconnected from curriculum outcomes
- Students receive marks but no insight into their own progress
- Accreditation preparation takes months instead of days

This is not a technology problem. It is a connection problem.

---

## SOLUTION SECTION

**Section header:**
Sapiion connects what should never have been separated.

**Body:**
Sapiion is an Educational Intelligence Platform built for Dutch professional education (HBO and MBO). It brings together everything that matters — in one place.

**Feature pillars (4 cards):**

### Curriculum Design
Design programmes that are alive — not locked in Word documents. Map learning outcomes to modules, assignments and rubric criteria. Ensure what you design is what gets taught.

### Teaching Delivery
Plan lessons, track attendance and manage classes from the same environment where the curriculum lives. Less administration. More time teaching.

### Assessment & Grading
Grade work using rubric criteria connected directly to learning outcomes. Every score becomes evidence of competency development — not just a number.

### Student Success & Intelligence
See, at any moment, which students need attention — before it is too late. Coordinators get real-time programme intelligence. Students see exactly where they stand.
