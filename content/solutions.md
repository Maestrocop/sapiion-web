### For Programme Managers

**Headline:**
Know whether your programme is delivering what it promises — before accreditation, not during it.

**Their reality:**
Sandra manages 24 teachers across 6 cohorts. She discovers student problems too late. Accreditation preparation takes months of collecting evidence that should already exist. She cannot see, at a glance, whether learning outcomes are being assessed consistently.

**What Sapiion gives Sandra:**

- **Programme health dashboard** — one view of the entire programme: student risk, outcome coverage, grade distribution, attendance trends
- **Continuous accreditation readiness** — every outcome linked, every assessment evidenced, every student tracked. The evidence is always there.
- **Early warning system** — coordinators see at-risk students weeks before they fail, not after
- **Curriculum consistency** — teachers work from shared rubrics and outcomes. No more every-teacher-does-it-differently.
- **Real-time visibility** — not an annual report. A live picture of programme health.

**The result:**
When accreditation comes, preparation takes days — not months. Student dropout decreases. Quality becomes institutional, not personal.

### For Coordinators and Educational Advisors

**Headline:**
Intervene before students fail. Not after.

**Their reality:**
Fatima manages 45 advisees. She discovers problems reactively — students come to her after things have gone wrong. Grades are in one system, attendance in another. BSA preparation is manual, document-heavy and stressful.

**What Sapiion gives Fatima:**

- **Complete student profile** — attendance, grades, outcome progress and risk signals in one view. No hunting across systems.
- **Risk signals** — see which students need attention this week, ranked by urgency
- **Progression review workflow** — structured, documented, defensible. Evidence collected automatically.
- **Advisor workload dashboard** — see all your advisees at once. Know who needs a call before they ask for one.
- **Intervention tracking** — log actions, set follow-up dates, track outcomes

**The result:**
Fatima arrives at every student meeting already knowing the full picture. BSA becomes a review process — not a collection exercise. She acts before students reach crisis point.

### For Teachers

**Headline:**
Less administration. More teaching. Students you actually know.

**Their reality:**
Martijn spends hours each week on tasks that have nothing to do with teaching. Grades go in one system, attendance in another. His rubrics are rebuilt from scratch every semester. He loses track of which students are struggling until it is too late.

**What Sapiion gives Martijn:**

- **One place for everything** — curriculum, classes, attendance, grading, student progress. One login.
- **Rubric library** — rubrics built once, connected to outcomes, reused across assignments. No rebuilding.
- **Attendance in one click** — register attendance directly from the class view. No separate system.
- **Student insight** — see, at a glance, which students are at risk across all your classes
- **Connected grading** — grades are tied to rubric criteria and outcomes. Every score means something.
- **Structured feedback** — give students feedback tied to specific criteria, not just a number

**The result:**
Martijn saves hours every week on administration. He knows his students before they have to come to him. His grading builds evidence — not just marks.

### For Students

Know exactly where you stand. Before it becomes a problem.

**Their reality:**

**What Sapiion gives Amir:**

- **Outcome progress** — see which competencies he is developing. Not just scores — real progress.
- **Rubric criteria before submission** — he knows what "good" looks like before he submits, not after

### For ICT Managers

**Headline:**
One platform that reduces complexity. Not one more system to manage.

**Their reality:**
Peter is responsible for infrastructure, security and GDPR. He has seen too many software projects fail. He has too many systems already. He needs answers before he says yes.

**What Sapiion gives Peter:**

- **Single platform** — Sapiion replaces multiple disconnected tools. Fewer licences, fewer integrations, less complexity.
- **EU hosting** — data stored in the EU. No ambiguity about data residency.
- **Data isolation** — each institution's data is completely separate. No shared databases.
- **GDPR documentation** — Data Processing Agreement, privacy policy and security documentation ready before contract signing
- **Data export** — full data export at any time. No lock-in.
- **Modern architecture** — built on PostgreSQL, Node.js, React. No legacy system.
- **Integration roadmap** — Microsoft 365 and SSO integration on the roadmap (honest timeline provided)

**The result:**
Peter can sign the DPA with confidence. His team is not managing a ghost system — adoption is high because every role gets genuine value from day one.
