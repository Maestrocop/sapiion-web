## FAQ (on this page)

**How long does implementation take?**
Sapiion is designed for a structured 90-day onboarding. Your programme does not stop while you implement — it improves while you do.

**What does it cost?**
Sapiion is priced per institution, not per user. We discuss pricing during or after the demo — once we understand your programme size and needs.

**Is our data safe and GDPR-compliant?**
Yes. Sapiion is hosted in the EU. Each institution's data is completely isolated. We provide a full Data Processing Agreement before any contract is signed.

**Do you integrate with Canvas / Brightspace / Osiris?**
Our integration roadmap includes Microsoft 365 and SSO as priorities. Speak with us about your specific integration requirements during the demo.

**Can we export our data if we leave?**
Yes. Full data export is always available. No lock-in.

**What happens after the pilot?**
After a pilot, we review what worked, what needs adjustment, and agree on a full rollout plan together.
