## What Sapiion Is

Sapiion is not an LMS. It is not a SIS. It is not a reporting tool.

Sapiion is an **Education Intelligence Platform** — the operating system that connects curriculum design, teaching, assessment, student success, and institutional intelligence into one continuous improvement cycle.

It helps educational institutions answer the questions that matter:
- Are we delivering the curriculum as designed?
- Which students need intervention — before it is too late?
- Which learning outcomes are never mastered?
- Where are our quality risks?
- Are we accreditation-ready?
- Which modules need redesign?
